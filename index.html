WHO WE ARE

The Patriots Gala was born from a simple, powerful belief: patriotism deserves memory. Each year, we gather to pause, to reflect, and to honour the individuals whose acts of courage, leadership, and loyalty keep the Nigerian spirit alive. They are the men and women — young and old — who stand tall when it matters, who lead not for praise but for purpose. This is more than an award show. It is a promise to never forget. A vow to spotlight those who serve with dignity and inspire with their example. It is our tribute to the past, our gratitude in the present, and our hope for the future. Because a nation that remembers its heroes becomes great.
